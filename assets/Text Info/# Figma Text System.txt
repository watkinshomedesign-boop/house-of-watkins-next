## Summary

### Font Family
All text styles use **Gilroy** as the primary font family.

### Weight Distribution
- **Titles**: Semibold (600)
- **Buttons**: Semibold (600) 
- **Body**: Regular (400)

### Letter Spacing
- **Titles**: 0%
- **Buttons**: 1%
- **Body**: 0%

### Text Transform
No uppercase or other text transforms are applied at the style level.

## Notes

- The Gilroy font has a warning icon in Figma, indicating it may be missing or need to be installed
- Line heights are specified in pixels
- Letter spacing is specified as percentage
- All styles have consistent naming: [Category]/[Size] format
